Q: State the first DeMorgan Law
A:

$$
\neg \left( P \land Q \right) \equiv \neg P \lor \neg Q
$$


Q: What is the inverse of a 2x2 matrix?
A:

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{pmatrix}^{-1} = \frac{1}{ad - bc} \begin{pmatrix}
d & -b \\
-c & a
\end{pmatrix}
$$
