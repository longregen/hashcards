Q: What is the capital of France?
A: Paris, known as the **City of Light**, is the capital and most populous city of France. It lies on the River Seine.

Q: What is the longest river in the world?
A: The **Nile River**, stretching approximately 6,650 km (4,130 mi) through northeastern Africa.
